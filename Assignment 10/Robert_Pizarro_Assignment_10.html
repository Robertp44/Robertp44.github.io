New York City

Situated on one of the worlds largest natural harbors, New York City consists of five boroughs. each of which is a seperate county of the state of New York. The five boroughs - Brooklyn, Queens, Manhattan, The Bronx and Staten Island - were consolidated into a single city in 1898.
